Layout notes (practice)

  • O₂/EAN32 racks: ≥6.1 m from any fuel or ignition source, or a ≥1.5 m high 30-min fire barrier.
  • Cascade racks, booster and filtration panel are WALL-MOUNTED/wall-backed — never free-standing in open floor. Cylinders chained at ⅓ and ⅔ height; storage ≤52 °C.
  • Forced ventilation is mandatory >15 kW: ~400 m³/h per kW at ΔT 7 K. Capture-at-source: hood over the compressors + HVAC duct outdoors (≤5 m/s duct velocity) beats room dilution; ducting the Kaeser's own exhaust removes ~21 kW more.
  • Fill whips: use a containment fill station (fragments directed upward); cylinder axes point at blank masonry, never at the door or operator.
  • Egress ≥0.71 m at every point; door ≥0.81 m clear, opens outward; ≥2.03 m headroom under pipe runs on the escape path.
  • Membrane N₂-rich retentate and all relief/vent lines terminate outdoors.
  • Electrical DB (~50 kW load) needs 0.91×0.76 m clear working space, outside the O₂ bay.
