How it works

1Air production (LP)

Ambient air is compressed by the Kaeser oil-injected screw K-01 to 10–13 bar, then cooled and stripped of bulk water (SEP, auto-drain), dried in the refrigerated dryer D-01 and polished by the coalescing pair F-01 A/B. The receiver R-01 buffers the two LP consumers: membrane feed and booster drive air. (Drying only works on compressed air — compression concentrates the water vapour so cooling can condense it out; that is why the dryer sits after K-01, never on ambient air.) PSV-01 protects the LP side.

2EANx membrane

PCV-01 sets a stable feed pressure and H-01 holds the feed at ~45 °C (TI-01) — membrane cut is temperature-sensitive. The AC guard bed F-03 protects the fibres from oil. Throttling the nitrogen-rich retentate at NV-01 sets the product O₂%; the oxygen-enriched permeate is monitored by AT-01, which trips the HP compressors at ≥40 % O₂. At the HP suction, V-01 admits membrane product; for plain-air fills V-02 opens the compressors' own ambient intake instead — air never needs the LP train, and its moisture is removed after compression by E-01, the separator and F-02.

3HP compression & purification

Two Bauer PE 350 compressors C-01/C-02 (350 lpm each, N+1) compress to 300 bar through discharge check valves. The chiller E-01 cools the final stage so the HP separator and the filtration towers work efficiently. F-02 — three Bauer P61 towers (molecular sieve / activated carbon / hopcalite) plus a P41 final tower carrying the Securus sensor MT-01 — is held above 150 bar by the pressure-maintaining valve PMV-01; AT-02 spot-checks CO against EN 12021.

4Storage cascades

Purified product routes to the air cascade BANK-A or the EAN32 cascade BANK-N depending on what is being produced — each bank group has its own isolation valve, gauge and relief, with non-return valves preventing back-flow toward the compressors. A direct line (V-08) feeds the panel for topping while banks recover. Fill from the lowest-pressure bank first; cascade upward.

5O₂ / He and the booster

The oxygen cascade (4×50 L) and helium cascade (2×50 L) feed the panel two ways: regulated low-pressure legs (PCV-03/04) for partial-pressure blending into near-empty cylinders, and the air-driven booster BST-01 (USUN GBD40-OL, drive set by PCV-02) when the target pressure exceeds bottle pressure. Slow-opening valves, dedicated suction valves per gas and a purge point keep the transfer O₂-safe; everything inside the dashed boundary is cleaned for oxygen service per ASTM G93 / CGA G-4.1.

6Filling & blending panel

Every inlet of FP-01 carries isolation, a check valve and an inline filter. O₂ and He are metered through needle valves against the master gauge PI-11. A restricted analysis line (FO-01) feeds the Deox multi-gas analyzer AT-03 — O₂, He and CO₂ read on every fill — and the verified mix gets a printed contents label (LBL-01). The manifold has its own relief (PSV-05) and vent; four whips with individual isolation and bleed valves serve the diving cylinders.

General notes

  1. All components inside the dashed boundary are cleaned for oxygen service per ASTM G93 / CGA G-4.1 — hydrocarbon-free, O₂-compatible seats and lubricants, slow-opening valves on all high-pressure O₂ isolation points.
  2. System design pressure 300 bar; all HP relief valves set at 330 bar (10 % accumulation). LP side design 13 bar, relief 16 bar.
  3. EAN32 storage and every line carrying >23.5 % O₂ is treated as oxygen service.
  4. All PSV discharges, the N₂-rich retentate, booster drive exhaust, purge and panel vents route outdoors above roof level, away from the compressor intake.
  5. Condensate auto-drains (AD) collect to an oil–water separator before disposal.
  6. Interlocks: AT-01 ≥40 % O₂ trips HP compressors · MT-01 saturation trips compressors · PMV-01 guarantees filtration efficiency.
  7. Informational — not licence-grade. Sizing estimates only, not a regulatory or licensing determination.
